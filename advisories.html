Access to OpenCVE gives you alerts on new vulnerabilities. It lets you search the CVE filtered by vendor, product, CVSS or CWE. You can also get notified of newly found vulnerabilities. The data is synchronized with the feed provided by the NVD.

The data from OpenCVE is freely accessible to all. Feel free to reach out to cudeso.be if you have specific notification needs or are interested in threat landscape reports for your ICS sector.
